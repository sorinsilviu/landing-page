This website is about tanks from Romanian Armed Forces

This website has a short description about 3 of the tanks in the Romanian Army

The TR-85 is a main battle tank designed for the armed forces of Romania. Based on the TR-77-580, the TR-85 tank was developed from 1978 to 1985 and produced from 1986 until 1990.

TR-580 is a Romanian tank designed during the Cold War. Approximately 400 tanks were made.

T-55 was a Soviet main battle tank introduce after the Second World War. Romania currently has around 120 of these tanks
